Thunderdome Data

What is Thunderdome data?

This is the last resting place for the work of the data team at Digital First Media's Project Thunderdome. Our team--comprised of Peggy Bustamante, Vaughn Hagerty, Nelson Hsu, Tom Meagher and MaryJo Webster--built interactive graphics and news applications and supported computer-assisted reporting projects for the nation's second largest newspaper company. In the spring of 2014, the company decided to shutter Thunderdome and the data team along with it. Before we left, we went back and open sourced many of the projects that we built over the 21 months of our existence.

What is all of this code?

These are charts and maps, graphs and quizes, news applications and widgets that ran on the websites of Digital First Media's 75 daily papers. Many of these we created as a result of partnering with reporters and editors in those local newsrooms. Others.

Most of these are just the code for the projects, including X, Y, Z.

A handful of these are tools and templates that we found ourselves using over and over again. We think these projects in particular will be of interest to other journalists.
